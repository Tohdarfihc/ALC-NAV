What is ALC Navigator?

ALC Navigator works by scouring public records at states and LGAs levels to find
information about specific addresses for 3MTT ALC.
Using building records and other public databases, advanced algorithms source the
most up-to-date information about Advanced Learning Centers for 3MTT.

Our search engine pulls a wealth of data from a wide range of sources that include:

  • Public Records
  • 3MTT Records
  • Social media

ABOUT

The Federal Ministry of Communications, Innovation, and Digital Economy presents the 3MTT training program, a 3-month initiative to equip participants with cutting-edge digital skills. To ensure seamless access to our Applied Learning Clusters (ALCs), we introduce the ALC Navigator website.

Overcoming Location Barriers

We understand that finding the nearest ALC can be daunting, especially for those relocating or unfamiliar with local areas. Our platform bridges this gap, providing:

  • Locations of various 3MTT Applied Learning Clusters
  • Contact details for easy communication
  • Directions to ensure hassle-free access
Your Gateway to Digital Excellence

With ALC Navigator, you can:

  • Locate the nearest ALC instantly
  • Plan your training schedule efficiently
  • Connect with fellow participants and mentors
  • Access digital resources and support
